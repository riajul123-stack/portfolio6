About Us

Khondker Builders

Since 2014, Khondker Builders has been at the forefront of Bangladesh’s infrastructure and agricultural development. With a strong commitment to excellence, we specialize in railway track construction and the supply of essential railway equipment, helping to strengthen one of the nation’s most vital transport networks.

Beyond railways, we proudly partner with the Government of Bangladesh on agricultural projects, contributing to sustainable growth and modernization in the farming sector. Our work reflects a vision of progress—combining technical expertise, innovation, and reliability to build solutions that serve communities and drive national development.

At Khondker Builders, we believe in building more than structures—we build trust, efficiency, and a better future for Bangladesh.
